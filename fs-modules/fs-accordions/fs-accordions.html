When is permit parking enforced?
 

Permits are required Monday-Friday from 7 a.m. to 4 p.m. Meter areas are enforced from 7 a.m. to 4 p.m. During campus breaks and in between semesters the “W” lots do not require a permit to park. The “A” lots are enforced year round.

Virtual Permit Holders: Be sure to park so that your license plate is visible from the travel lane.

Temporary Permit Holders: Be sure to display your hang tag on your rearview mirror.
