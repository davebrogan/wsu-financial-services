Alternate Transportation

UTA OGX Route (Wildcat Express)

Ogden Bus Rapid Transit (OGX) has three stops on the WSU Ogden campus: at the Dee Events Center, Wildcat Village and Central Campus near the Browning Center and Shepherd Union Building. Shuttles run every 10 minutes and are free.

Intercampus Lyft Service

Weber State University has partnered with Lyft to provide 75% off rides between Ogden Campus and Davis Campus . Discounted rides must have an on campus pick-up and drop-off location and will be available Monday-Friday, 6a.m.-10p.m.

UTA Ed Pass

Weber State University has entered into an agreement with Utah Transit Authority. Under this agreement WSU faculty, staff and students are able to ride UTA buses by showing a current UTA Ed Pass card. Ski buses are not included in this program.

The Ed pass is available at the Student Union Information Desk. Ed Pass pricing and FAQ's.

Car Pool

WSU Parking Services is engaged in educating the campus community concerning alternative transportation options. In the coming years it is essential for us to reduce the number of single occupancy vehicles, enhance air quality and converse energy.

Looking for others near you that want to join a carpool? UTA offers a carpool match program. www.utacommuter.com

Bike to Campus

Ride your bike to campus. There are bike racks located all over campus. See map for rack locations as well as dismount areas.
