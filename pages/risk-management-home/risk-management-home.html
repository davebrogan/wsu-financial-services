Risk Management

The Department of Risk Management assists the university in achieving its goals of education and service in an advisory and consultative capacity with regard to assessing risks, insurance coverage, contract language specific to insurance requirements, and acceptable levels of risk.

In partnership with the State of Utah Division of Risk Management, we ensure university assets are protected and timely recovery of insured losses.

 

When do you need to consult with us?

There are lots of reasons to contact risk management, and we are happy to answer your questions. 

It is important that you reach out so that we can help you:

  • Assess risks involved in your project or travel. 
  • Complete the necessary forms for accident reporting. 
  • Plan travel that includes students
  • Safely include minors on campus
  • Travel out of the country with or without students. 
  • Get permission to fly a drone on campus.

We can help you check all the necessary boxes to protect all involved. When in doubt, give us a call. It’s easier to plan and prepare ahead than to fix things after they happen.

Insurance Providers

Weber State University General Liability, Professional Liability, Errors and Omissions, and Automobile Liability insurance is provided through the State of Utah Risk Management Fund.

University employees are insured for work related injuries or illnesses through the Worker’s Compensation Fund of Utah.
