How to Purchase Permits “A” Permit Lottery Refunds
A

A Permit: $145 per year, $86 per semester
 

A lottery determines which seniors and veterans can purchase “A” permits. Any permits not picked up will be offered to underclassmen. Students are eligible for these permits on a first-come, first-served basis. Permit holder must park in the designated “A” parking lot or in any “W” or “R” parking lot and at the Davis Campus or Dee Events Center.

Available for Purchase July 1, 2024.

W

W Permit: $95 per year, $58 per semester
 

"W" Permits may be purchased at any time. Allows holder to park in any "W" parking lot and the Davis Campus.

R/W

R/W Permit: $95 per year, $58 per semester
 

"RW" permits can ONLY be purchased by housing residents and adjoining tenants. Allows holder to park in any "W" lots, in the "R" parking lot(s) near their residence, and the Davis Campus.

R

R Permit: $67 per year, $40 per semester
 

"R" permits can ONLY be purchased by housing residents and adjoining tenants. Allows holder to park in the "R" parking lot(s) near their residence only.

‌

Disability Permit: $95 per year, $58 per semester
 

Register with parking services to purchase a disability permit. Display state placard with registered license plate. Valid in any lot, except reserved stalls, meters and public pay lot. Individuals with temporary disabilities can obtain a temporary disability permit at the cost of $12 per week for a maximum period of 2 weeks.

M

When is permit parking enforced?
 

Riding a motorcycle or moped is one of the easiest and most economical ways to get to campus. Not only do motorcycles reduce traffic congestion, but parking is much easier than for an automobile. There is no permit required to park motorcycles on either campus, however, you must park your motorcycle in the designated motorcycle area. These areas are typically found at the end of parking rows and have green hash marks on the pavement. Motorcycles may not use automobile spaces except at paid meters.
